Who We Are

A Lebanese Firm with a Regional Presence

Founded in Lebanon and active across the Middle East, MIGERA is a consulting and management firm that works closely with businesses at every stage of their development. Our work spans hospitality consulting, property investment in Georgia, and the growth of the Meetup Coffee Shop brand.

We call ourselves Caring Partners because that is precisely what we are. We take genuine interest in the outcome of every engagement, and we remain involved long after the initial brief is complete.
