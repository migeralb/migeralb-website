The Opportunity

Why Invest in Georgia?

Georgia has become one of the most straightforward and rewarding property markets for foreign investors. The legal framework is clear, ownership rights are well protected, and the combination of tourism growth and urban development in Tbilisi and Batumi has driven consistent price appreciation.

We work directly with our clients through the entire process, from the initial enquiry through to completion. All of our property advisory is available in Arabic and English.

  • ✦No property tax for individuals, making Georgia one of the most tax-efficient markets for foreign buyers
  • ✦Strong rental demand in both Tbilisi and Batumi, supported by growing international tourism
  • ✦Straightforward ownership laws with full property rights available to foreign nationals
  • ✦Property values in key districts have appreciated consistently over recent years
  • ✦Flexible payment plans available directly through York Towers
  • ✦Bilingual support in Arabic and English throughout the purchase process

Where We Invest

Prime Locations in Georgia

🏙

Tbilisi — The Capital

The capital and commercial centre of Georgia. Tbilisi has undergone significant development in recent years, with new infrastructure, a growing international community, and strong demand for quality residential property.

🌊

Batumi — The Black Sea Riviera

Located on the Black Sea coast, Batumi is Georgia's principal resort city. Its combination of year-round tourism, modern development, and active short-term rental market makes it a strong choice for income-focused investors.

🌿

Saguramo — Nature & Villas

A peaceful retreat 30 minutes from Tbilisi. York Towers' villa development offers private luxury in nature — ideal for permanent residency or premium vacation rental income.

🏔

The pristine Bazaleti Lake region north of Tbilisi. A growing eco-tourism destination with York Towers offering a unique resort investment in exceptional natural surroundings.
