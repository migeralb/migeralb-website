The Opportunity

Why Invest in Georgia?

Georgia has rapidly emerged as one of the most attractive real estate markets in the world — combining strong ROI, minimal taxes, simple ownership laws for foreigners, and a booming tourism sector.

As MIGERA's authorized broker, we make investing in York Towers seamless for Lebanese and Arab investors — with full Arabic and English support throughout.

  • ✦0% property tax for individuals — one of the world's most investor-friendly regimes
  • ✦Strong rental yields driven by booming tourism in Tbilisi and Batumi
  • ✦Simple, transparent property ownership laws open to all foreign nationals
  • ✦Rapidly appreciating property values in emerging prime districts
  • ✦Flexible payment plans and mortgage packages available through York Towers
  • ✦Full Arabic-speaking support via MIGERA from enquiry to handover
